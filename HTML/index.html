Who is Rafiul Prodhan?



Hello, my name is Rafiul and I am a serial entrepreneur.
I have launched 4 businesses in the course of 2 years all of which have been profitable.
I was able to quit my 9 to 5 and able to buy my time back to pursue my passions.
My passion for learning and manifesting ideas, my goal is to motivate others to do the same.

Donut Booths is a party and entertainment company that specializes in the social photography experience.
We provide photobooth and decorative wall services in the Tri-state area.

Donut Booths was founded in November 2019 with my wife while we were vacationing in Bangladesh and planning our first business venture.
